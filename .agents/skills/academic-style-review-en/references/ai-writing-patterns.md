# AI-Writing Pattern Checklist

Flag these patterns for review:

- Generic importance openers.
- Overuse of `moreover`, `furthermore`, `additionally`.
- Excessive em dashes.
- Uniform paragraph length.
- Repetitive paragraph-final summary sentences.
- Vague nouns: factors, aspects, issues, considerations.
- Inflated adjectives: groundbreaking, comprehensive, robust, novel, significant.
- Corporate verbs: leverage, utilize, facilitate, operationalize.
- Unsupported universal claims.
- Synonym cycling for the same concept.

## Revision strategy

- Replace vague phrases with concrete mechanisms or evidence.
- Delete filler phrases.
- Weaken unsupported claims.
- Preserve technical terminology.
- Keep deliberate repetition when it improves clarity.
