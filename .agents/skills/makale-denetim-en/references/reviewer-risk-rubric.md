# Reviewer Risk Rubric

## P0: Likely rejection

- Core claim unsupported.
- Method cannot reproduce results.
- Data leakage likely.
- Baselines unfair or missing for central claim.
- Citations fabricated or unusable.
- Results contradict the contribution claim.

## P1: Major revision risk

- Motivation not connected to method.
- Evaluation incomplete.
- Ablation missing for claimed module.
- Threats to validity generic.
- Results interpreted too broadly.
- Related work misses a key research line.

## P2: Moderate clarity risk

- Paragraph flow weak.
- Terms inconsistent.
- Tables/figures not explained.
- Section structure hard to follow.
- Abstract omits key result.

## P3: Minor issue

- Local wording.
- Formatting inconsistency.
- Caption clarity.
- Citation style inconsistency.
