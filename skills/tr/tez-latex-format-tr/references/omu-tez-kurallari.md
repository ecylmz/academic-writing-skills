# OMU Thesis Formatting Rule Summary

Working summary: A4 paper; 4 cm left margin and 2.5 cm other margins; bottom-centered page numbers; Roman numerals before main text; Times New Roman 12 pt body; 14 pt main headings; 1.5 body line spacing; single spacing in summaries, lists, references, and CV; chapter-numbered figures, tables, and equations; table captions above tables; figure captions below figures; references alphabetized and consistently styled. Preserve local template behavior when present.
